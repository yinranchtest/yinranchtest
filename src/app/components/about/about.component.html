Luxury on the Edge of Yellowstone National Park

MTNLUX Yellowstone Getaway is a beautiful mountain lodge located at the base of Mount Republic near Cooke City Montana. This Mountain Refuge can be booked as one grand facility or individually as 3 private chalets. Warm, spacious and comfortable rooms let visitors enjoy togetherness yet still maintain the option to find quiet time apart. Strategically located on boarder of Yellowstone National Park, surrounded by the Beartooth Wilderness, this Luxury Mountain Refuge is an outdoor enthusiast dream.

We strive to be responsive and accomodating to ensure your stay at MTNLUX has all the comforts of home in the majesty of the high country.

Michael and Michelle Rosengren, Your Hosts
